# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Primarily sagelga himself — a personal documentation hub and reference library for his own projects. Occasional visitors (developers, potential collaborators, recruiters) who land here and form an impression of sagelga's technical taste and depth. The primary job: find and read technical docs. The secondary job: convey that this is the work of someone with craft and standards.

### Brand Personality
Personal, expressive, crafted — feels authored by a person with taste, not assembled from a template. Has genuine character and a clear point of view. Not corporate, not generic, not cold. Should feel like walking into someone's well-organized studio, not a SaaS landing page.

Three words: **authored, precise, warm**

### Aesthetic Direction
Reference: stripe.com / supabase.com — serious technical documentation with strong visual hierarchy. Clean and authoritative without being sterile. Content-first but with an unmistakable visual identity.

Brand palette: Indigo `#3B4A8C` + Amber `#C9943A` on warm dark canvas `#1A1814`. IBM Plex Sans Thai as body/heading, JetBrains Mono for code. The warmth in the dark palette is the signature — lean into it.

Anti-reference: Generic SaaS docs with centered hero, identical card grids, equal spacing everywhere, no rhythm.

### Design Principles
1. **Authored, not automated** — every spacing decision should feel deliberate. No value is arbitrary. Variety creates personality.
2. **Hierarchy through contrast** — space, weight, and color used together to create unmistakable rank. Never equal-weight.
3. **Warm technical** — the indigo/amber/charcoal palette is precise but not cold. Let the warmth breathe through generous whitespace and soft transitions.
4. **Rhythm over uniformity** — tight groupings within sections, generous air between them. The beat of the page should be felt, not just seen.
5. **Content leads, chrome follows** — decoration only where it serves. The sidebar, cards, and typography do the heavy lifting.
